Off The Streets Initiative

Street Comics - Our Stories Are Ours To Write

This is the street comic initiative. The Street Comic Initiative came from an idea. I’ve nurtured it, cultivated it, and I prepared it for this day. I plan to create some of the best content with everything that I am, and everything that I have. It is in an investment in what comes next. It is an investment into a future in which we create our own stories. It’s about turning passion into action and showing that you don’t need much to make something great. I want to inspire and encourage other creators because I love creating, and I want others to feel that same spark. Through this initiative, I’m self-publishing my first book, working diligently on a webcomic, and planning to explore other forms of content as time goes on. But right now, it all starts here... off the street.
